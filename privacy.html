Privacy Policy

Covered by Greco — Quote Tool & CRM. Last updated: April 25, 2026.

Covered by Greco is operated by Emanuel Greco, an independent licensed health insurance broker. This page describes how the Quote Tool & CRM web application (the “Service”) handles personal data.

1. Who this policy covers

The Service is used internally by Emanuel Greco and authorized assistants to quote ACA health plans and manage client relationships. End-clients of the brokerage are not direct users of the Service — they receive quotes and policy documents through standard channels (email, SMS, phone) and their information is entered into the Service by Emanuel on their behalf.

2. Information we collect

  • Client information you enter into the CRM: name, contact details (phone, email, address, ZIP), date of birth, household income, lead source, notes, and uploaded documents.
  • Quote data: ZIP code lookups, household composition, and ACA plan results returned by the federal Marketplace API.
  • Google account data (optional): if you choose to connect Google, the Service requests read & write access to your primary calendar (to sync CRM tasks as Calendar events) and read-only access to your Gmail metadata and message bodies (to display recent email threads with each contact inside the CRM).

3. How we use this data

  • Client data is used solely to operate the brokerage — quoting plans, scheduling follow-ups, tracking renewals, and communicating with the client.
  • Google Calendar data is used to create, update, and remove events that mirror tasks scheduled in the CRM.
  • Gmail data is used only to display threads inline with the corresponding CRM contact. Email contents are not stored on our servers; they are fetched on-demand from your Gmail account each time the CRM is opened.

4. How data is stored

Client records are stored in a private Supabase Postgres database with row-level security enabled. Only the authenticated brokerage account can read or modify the records. Uploaded documents are stored in a private Supabase Storage bucket scoped to the same account. Data is encrypted in transit (HTTPS / TLS) and at rest (Supabase / GCP managed storage).

5. Sharing

Client data is never sold, traded, rented, or shared with third parties for marketing purposes. Data is only disclosed to:

  • Health insurance carriers and the federal Marketplace, when submitting an enrollment on behalf of a client (with the client’s explicit authorization).
  • Government agencies, when required by law (e.g., HIPAA-related subpoenas).

6. Google API services — limited use

The Service’s use of information received from Google APIs adheres to the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements. Specifically, Google account data:

  • Is used only to provide user-facing features within the Service (calendar sync and email-thread display).
  • Is not used for advertising or transferred to third parties.
  • Is not used for AI/ML training.
  • Is not read by any human other than the account holder, except as required for security investigations or legal compliance.

7. Revoking access

You can disconnect Google at any time by visiting https://myaccount.google.com/permissions and removing “GripLeads CRM”. You can also clear stored CRM data by signing out of the Service.

8. Retention

CRM client records are retained for as long as the brokerage relationship is active and for a reasonable period thereafter for compliance with insurance recordkeeping requirements (typically 7 years). Google access tokens are stored only for the duration of an active session; they are not persisted to disk.

9. Children

The Service is not directed to children under 13 and does not knowingly collect their personal information except as listed dependents on a parent’s health insurance application.

10. Changes

If this policy is updated, the “Last updated” date at the top of the page will change.
